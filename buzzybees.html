Buzzy Bees are here!

Minecaft 1.15 is finally out! (officially called the Buzzy Bees Update) Mojang added bees, honey, bee nests, bee hives, honey blocks, honeycomb, honeycomb blocks, and lots of minor changes and bug fixes. For example, you can now heal damaged iron golems with iron ingots. Also, if you hit a bee it will get angry and sting you and die. (There's no point in killing bees. DON'T KILL THEM YOU MONSTER!) Bees will also make your crops grow faster if they pollinate them.
